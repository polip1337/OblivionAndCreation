# CP Realm Levels and Unlocks

Each level requires `100` total CP earned.

## Level 1-4: Qi Condensation

- Level 1 (Low): +1 inventory slot
- Level 2 (Mid): +1 inventory slot, Masters guidance +5%
- Level 3 (High): +1 inventory slot, Active Dao slot 2 can awaken early
- Level 4 (Peak): +1 inventory slot, Masters guidance +10%

## Level 5-8: Foundation Establishment

- Level 5 (Low): +2 inventory slots, Masters guidance +12%
- Level 6 (Mid): +2 inventory slots, Masters guidance +14%
- Level 7 (High): +2 inventory slots, Active Dao slot 3 can awaken early
- Level 8 (Peak): +2 inventory slots, Masters guidance +16%

## Level 9-12: Core Formation

- Level 9 (Low): +3 inventory slots, Masters guidance +18%
- Level 10 (Mid): +3 inventory slots, Masters guidance +20%
- Level 11 (High): +3 inventory slots, Masters guidance +22%
- Level 12 (Peak): +3 inventory slots, Masters guidance +24%

## Level 13-16: Nascent Soul

- Level 13 (Low): +4 inventory slots, Masters guidance +26%
- Level 14 (Mid): +4 inventory slots, Masters guidance +28%
- Level 15 (High): +4 inventory slots, Masters guidance +30%
- Level 16 (Peak): +5 inventory slots, Masters guidance +35%
